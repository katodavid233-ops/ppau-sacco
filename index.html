About PPAU SACCO

Saving Today, Securing Tomorrow

Building Financial Security for Pharmacy Professionals

The Pharmacy Professionals Association of Uganda (PPAU) SACCO was established in January 2026 following a unison resolution by the PPAU AGM 2025, with a shared vision of empowering pharmacy professionals — especially pharmacy technicians/dispensers and pharmacy assistants — through a strong culture of saving, responsible borrowing, and collective investment.

It was observed and recognized that while pharmacy professionals play a vital role in improving healthcare, many continue to face financial challenges that limit personal growth, professional advancement, and business development. We therefore came together to establish a member-owned financial institution that addresses these needs and creates lasting economic opportunities.

PPAU SACCO is more than a savings and credit cooperative; it is a platform for unity, financial discipline, and shared prosperity. By saving and investing together, we are building a financially resilient community where members have access to affordable credit, opportunities for wealth creation, and long-term financial security.

Through the SACCO, members can finance business ventures, establish pharmacies and drug shops, drug dispenseries, and also pursue further education, acquire assets, and achieve other life goals with confidence.

We believe that the success of our SACCO depends on the active participation, trust, and commitment of every member. Together, we are laying a strong foundation built on transparency, accountability, integrity, and sound governance to ensure that members' savings are managed prudently and that the SACCO continues to grow sustainably.

What We Offer

Our Services

Comprehensive financial solutions tailored for pharmacy professionals

Regular Savings

Build your savings with flexible monthly contributions. Earn competitive dividends on your savings at the end of each financial year.

  • Minimum monthly contribution: UGX 30,000
  • Competitive annual dividends
  • Withdraw anytime after 6 months

Salary Advance Loan

Access quick funds before your next payday. Available to members with at least 6 months of savings history.

  • Up to 50% of savings
  • 2% monthly interest rate
  • Repay within 3 months

Development Loan

Fund your personal development projects - home construction, land purchase, or home improvement. Available to members with at least 6 months of savings history.

  • Up to UGX 30,000,000
  • Up to 24 months repayment
  • 1.5% monthly interest

Business Expansion Loan

Grow your pharmacy business with our special business loan. Available to members with at least 6 months of savings history.

  • Up to UGX 50,000,000
  • Flexible repayment up to 36 months
  • Business plan support provided

Emergency Loan

Face unexpected financial challenges with confidence. Available to members with at least 6 months of savings history.

  • Quick approval within 48 hours
  • Up to UGX 5,000,000
  • Minimal documentation required

Financial Literacy Training

Free financial education workshops and resources to help members make informed financial decisions and build wealth.

  • Monthly financial literacy webinars
  • One-on-one financial coaching
  • Investment guidance resources

Mobile Banking

Access your SACCO account anytime, anywhere through our mobile platform. Check balances, make transfers, and apply for loans on the go.

  • USSD and Mobile App access
  • Real-time balance notifications
  • Mobile loan application

Group Lending

Borrow collectively with fellow pharmacy professionals. Group loans offer lower interest rates and shared accountability.

  • Groups of 5-10 members
  • Reduced interest rates
  • Peer support and accountability

Share Capital Investment

Invest in SACCO shares and become a co-owner. Shareholders receive annual share dividends and voting rights at the AGM.

  • Minimum 5 shares at UGX 20,000 each
  • Annual share dividend
  • Voting rights at AGM
Join Us

Become a Member

Join thousands of pharmacy professionals already benefiting from PPAU SACCO

Requirements

  • Must be registered with Allied Health Professionals Council and member of PPAU
  • Completed the SACCO membership application form
  • Submit two passport photos
  • Paid membership fees of UGX 20,000
  • Purchased a minimum of 5 shares each at UGX 20,000 (UGX 100,000)
  • Submit a copy of valid national ID or passport or driving license

Membership Benefits

  • Access to affordable loans at reduced rates
  • Annual dividends on savings and shares
  • Financial literacy training and workshops
  • Voting rights at Annual General Meeting
  • Emergency loan facility
  • Networking with fellow professionals
  • Group insurance benefits
